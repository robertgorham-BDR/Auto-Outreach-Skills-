# Notice

This repository is **internal sales enablement for the Testsigma BDR team**.

The methodology and playbook it contains are written to be public/marketing-safe, and the repo is public so teammates can clone it easily. That does **not** make it a general-purpose template for other companies or a place for company-confidential material.

Please keep it that way:

- **It is for Testsigma BDRs.** Configure it to yourself and run it on your own accounts.
- **Bring only your own data**, and keep it local. Never add a real prospect, customer, or teammate's contact details, another rep's lists, or any CRM/Apollo IDs or credentials. Those stay in your gitignored `config.json` and local files.
- **Each rep runs on their own data.** This kit does not share contacts, sent history, or pipeline between people.
- **When in doubt, leave it out.** If you're not sure something is safe to commit to a public repo, it isn't.

The `scripts/pii-scan` guard and the CI check exist to enforce this, but the responsibility is everyone's.
