# Run-my-day prompt — the morning driver

> Paste this each morning to get oriented and set up the day's Testsigma outreach. The agent reads your baked-in ICP, proof points, sender, and quota from the kit; config.json holds your Apollo login, sequences, and assigned accounts.

```
Run my day. In order:
1. Read CLAUDE.md, skills/_shared/outreach-gauntlet.md, and config.json.
2. Check my Apollo sequences and MASTER_SENT_LIST for DUE tasks first (T2/T3 follow-ups,
   LinkedIn steps coming due) — these are the cheapest, highest-priority sends. List them.
   Remember: due tasks are ADDITIONAL to any net-new quota, never a substitute for it.
3. Scan my inbox / Apollo replies for new ones; classify and surface any warm ones (positive /
   curious / referral) with a recommended response. Flag anything time-sensitive.
4. Check my warm-leads and pipeline files for follow-ups owed today.
5. Tell me what's due, plus any bounces to add to memory/do-not-contact.md and log.
6. Given my daily targets in config.json (emails / LinkedIn / calls) and my monthly SAL goal,
   recommend the day's priorities and one net-new batch to build from my assigned TAM slice
   (Factor / intent accounts first, then TAM HIGH), scoped to the Manager-and-above persona lock.

Surface it as a short briefing, then wait. Draft nothing and send nothing until I pick a task.
Nothing sends without my explicit APPROVE SEND, and every send gets logged to MASTER_SENT_LIST.csv.
```
